The aim of my bachelor thesis was to investigate the added value of integrating VR technology into the parametric design and planning methodology. For this purpose, theoretical foundations were collected, a comprehensive 3D model with VR function was planned and finally compared with other display media using an evaluation matrix. The result of the first steps of the investigation confirms the assumption that an immersive approach to checking and developing the design and planning creates a better viewing quality. The joint inspection of the virtual planning environment proves to be another advantage.
