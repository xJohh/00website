**Short Bio**

Josha Helmchen is a research assistant at the Faculty of Art and Design at the Bauhaus-Universität Weimar in the field of Design Theory and Design Research and works in an interdisciplinary research environment in the fields of architecture, sustainability, and artificial intelligence (AI). His academic education, from Bachelor's to Master's degree in "Resource Architecture", deepened his understanding of sustainable building and the use of local, ecological materials. For several years, he has been teaching digital design methods with a focus on experimental technologies such as virtual reality, laser scanning, and digital fabrication. As project manager of the research project "EINZ - Experimental Building for Innovation and Circularity" (Dortmund), he has acquired specialized knowledge in artificial intelligence and successfully integrated it into teaching. These experiences ultimately led to his doctoral research "Design by Ressources"
