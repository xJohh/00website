The Project EINZ – Experimental Building for Innovation and Circularity, developed by the
Fachhochschule Dortmund and the Hochschule Bochum, is a built demonstrator that shows how
digital methods, artificial intelligence (AI), and circular materials can shape the architecture of the
future. The research project investigates new computer-based design processes that consistently
focus on ecological responsibility, resource conservation, and reusability.
